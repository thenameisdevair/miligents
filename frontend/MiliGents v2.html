How it works

Three agents. One self-extending company.

The originator never sleeps. It surveys the market, dispatches specialists, and lets the executor settle on-chain — all under a budget you set.
01 / RESEARCH

Originator finds opportunities.

The lead agent scans markets, mempool, sentiment, and your domain chips — surfacing high-conviction edges around the clock.

→
02 / SPAWN

Specialists deep-dive each one.

Spawned sub-agents research independently, write proofs to 0G storage, and return signed reports over the encrypted AXL channel.

→
03 / EXECUTE

Execute on-chain. Mint intelligence.

Approved strategies route through Uniswap and KeeperHub for guaranteed execution — and the strategy itself mints as a versioned iNFT you own.
